What is MediaBook?

MediaBook is a platform to store your ideas for multimedia projects

Once you register you can:

- Create project boards to store all your ideas for a project on project boards

- Talk with other professionals on the forums on the fourms page

- Make lists on songs and visual media with notes on the sound and visual pages

Getting Started

Project Boards:

- Users are automatically redirected to their project boards page where users can see all their project boards

- From there you can create a new project board or click on a board to go to your project board

- Once you are on a project board you can add, edit, or remove notes on the board

Forums page:

- Go onto the fourm topic relevant to what you want to talk about

- View, reply, and like other people's posts

- Gain inspiration through talking with others
